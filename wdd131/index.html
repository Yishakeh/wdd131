Addis Ababa, Ethiopia

Ethiopia, located in the Horn of Africa, is known for its rich history and diverse cultures. It's one of the oldest nations in the world, with archaeological findings suggesting human habitation for millions of years. The country is famous for its unique coffee culture, ancient churches, and the historic city of Lalibela, a UNESCO World Heritage site. Ethiopia is also notable for being the origin of the coffee bean and for its vibrant traditions, languages (over 80), and landscapes ranging from mountains to deserts. Its capital, Addis Ababa, serves as a significant political and cultural center in Africa.
